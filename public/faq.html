Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ALI Charity and blockchain-based charitable giving.

ALI Charity is a blockchain-based charitable platform that ensures 100% transparency in donations. Every transaction is recorded on-chain, allowing donors to track exactly how their contributions are used.
All donations are recorded on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). You can verify every transaction using blockchain explorers like BscScan. No middlemen, no hidden fees, no manipulation.
We accept USDT, BTC, ETH, BNB, and SOL. All donations go to our transparent wallet address on the BSC network.
ALI Token is our native cryptocurrency. Donors receive ALI Tokens as rewards. Each ALI Token is pegged 1:1 with USDT and can be exchanged back at any time.
Zero platform fees. We only charge minimal blockchain network fees (gas fees) which are inherent to all crypto transactions.
After donating, you'll receive a unique transaction hash (TX Hash). You can paste this on BscScan.com to verify your donation on the blockchain.
