Everything you need to know about ALI Charity

ALI Charity is a blockchain-based charitable platform that ensures 100% transparency in donations. Every transaction is recorded on-chain, allowing donors to track exactly how their contributions are used.
All donations are recorded on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). You can verify every transaction using blockchain explorers like BscScan. No middlemen, no hidden fees, no manipulation.
We accept USDT, BTC, ETH, BNB, and SOL. All donations go to our transparent wallet address on the BSC network.
ALI Token is our native cryptocurrency. Donors receive ALI Tokens as rewards (1 ALI = 1 USDT). Token holders get governance rights and exclusive benefits. Plus, you get bonus rewards based on donation amount!
Zero platform fees. We only charge minimal blockchain network fees (gas fees) which are inherent to all crypto transactions. 100% of your donation goes to charity.
After donating, you'll receive a unique transaction hash (TX Hash). You can paste this on BscScan.com to verify your donation on the blockchain. It's permanent and immutable.
Based on your donation amount: $10-$99 gets +10% bonus, $100-$999 gets +20% bonus, $1,000+ gets +30% bonus. For example, donate $100 and receive 120 ALI tokens!
