How to Choose the Best Platform

1. Transparency Requirements

Consider how much visibility you want:

  • Maximum: Choose platforms with 100% on-chain verification like ALI Charity
  • High: UN-backed organizations with regular reporting
  • Standard: Traditional charities with third-party audits

2. Supported Cryptocurrencies

Ensure the platform accepts your crypto holdings:

  • Stablecoins: USDT, USDC (stable value)
  • Major: BTC, ETH (high recognition)
  • DeFi: BNB, SOL (low fees)

3. Fee Structure

Understand total costs:

  • Network transaction fees
  • Platform fees
  • Conversion fees (if applicable)
  • Administrative costs

4. Impact Focus

Choose causes you care about:

  • Global humanitarian aid

  • Healthcare
  • Environment
  • Education
