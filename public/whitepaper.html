📄 ALI Token Whitepaper

Complete documentation of the ALI Charity ecosystem

🎯 Executive Summary

ALI Charity is a revolutionary blockchain-based charitable platform that ensures 100% transparency in donations. Every transaction is recorded on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), allowing donors to track their funds in real-time.

The ALI Token serves as the platform's native utility token, offering governance rights, profit sharing, and exclusive benefits to donors. With a fixed 1:1 exchange rate to USDT, ALI tokens provide stability and immediate utility within the ecosystem.
