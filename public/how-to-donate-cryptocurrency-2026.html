Step-by-Step Guide to Donating Crypto

1 Set Up a Cryptocurrency Wallet

If you don't already have a crypto wallet, here's how to get one:

  1. Download MetaMask (desktop) or Trust Wallet (mobile) from their official websites
  2. Create a new wallet and choose a strong password
  3. Write down your 12-word seed phrase and store it securely offline
  4. Confirm your seed phrase to complete setup

Time required: 5-10 minutes

2 Fund Your Wallet

You'll need cryptocurrency in your wallet to donate. Here's how to get it:

  1. Create an account on a cryptocurrency exchange (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken)
  2. Complete identity verification (KYC)
  3. Purchase cryptocurrency using bank transfer or credit card
  4. Withdraw to your wallet address

Tip: BNB Smart Chain transactions have very low fees, making it ideal for donations.

3 Visit ALI Charity

Go to https://minadoai.com and browse verified charitable causes.

Unlike traditional charities, ALI Charity offers:

  • ✅ 0% platform fees - 100% of your donation reaches the cause
  • ✅ Real-time blockchain tracking
  • ✅ Verified causes only
  • ✅ ALI token rewards for donors

4 Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Donate" button on your chosen cause.

When prompted, connect your wallet using:

  • MetaMask - Click the MetaMask extension
  • WalletConnect - Scan QR code with your mobile wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet - Click the Coinbase option

5 Choose Cause and Enter Amount

Select:

  1. Cryptocurrency: BTC, ETH, BNB, USDT, USDC, or SOL
  2. Amount: Any amount you wish to donate
  3. Cause: Your chosen verified charitable organization
💡 Pro Tip: On ALI Charity, even small donations make a big impact. The 0% fees mean your full donation goes to work—no administrative costs.

6 Confirm the Transaction

Review your donation details and confirm:

  1. Check the cause name and donation amount
  2. Note the small blockchain gas fee (usually less than $1)
  3. Click "Confirm" or "Send"
  4. Approve the transaction in your wallet

That's it! Your donation is now recorded on the blockchain.

7 Track Your Impact

One of the best parts of donating crypto is complete transparency:

  1. Copy your transaction hash (TX hash) from the confirmation
  2. Visit bscscan.com (for BNB, USDT, USDC) or etherscan.io (for ETH)
  3. Paste your TX hash to verify the transaction
  4. Watch your donation arrive and track its use in real-time

Tax Benefits of Crypto Donations

One of the biggest advantages of donating cryptocurrency is the tax treatment. In the United States and many other countries:

Key Tax Advantages

  1. Avoid Capital Gains: Donate appreciated crypto without selling it, avoiding capital gains taxes
  2. Full Fair Market Value: Claim deduction for the full current value, not just what you paid
  3. Lower Tax Bracket Impact: Charitable deductions can lower your overall tax burden

⚠️ Consult a Tax Professional

Tax laws vary by country and individual circumstances. Always consult with a qualified tax professional before making donation decisions.
