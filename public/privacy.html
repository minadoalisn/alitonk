Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 3, 2026

ALI Charity ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://minadoai.com (the "Site") and use our blockchain-based charity services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Blockchain & Wallet Information

When you interact with our platform, the following information may be automatically collected from the blockchain:

  • Wallet address (public blockchain address)
  • Transaction history and donation records on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC)
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps
  • Smart contract interactions with our ALI Token contract

Note: Blockchain transactions are publicly visible by nature. We cannot control how third parties use publicly available blockchain data.

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Wallet connection data when you use our Connect Wallet feature
  • Contact information if you reach out to us via email or forms
  • Any information you voluntarily submit in connection with donations or community participation

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or source
  • Device information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and record your cryptocurrency donations on-chain
  • Issue ALI Tokens as donation rewards
  • Display transparent donation records and project funding progress
  • Communicate with you about your donations or platform updates
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Our platform is built on the principle of full transparency. All donations are recorded on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and are publicly verifiable at BScScan. The following contract addresses are used:

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With our charity project partners (only donation amounts and project allocation, not personal identity unless voluntarily provided)
  • With blockchain analytics providers for transaction verification
  • When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Smart contract security audits
  • Access controls and secure storage practices

However, no internet transmission or blockchain transaction is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential site functionality
  • Google Analytics to understand site traffic
  • Google Ads conversion tracking

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with:

  • Binance Smart Chain (BSC) - blockchain transactions
  • MetaMask & Web3 Wallets - wallet connection
  • CoinGecko API - cryptocurrency price data
  • Binance API - market data

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. International Transfers

Our platform is accessible globally. If you are accessing from outside your country, you consent to the transfer of your information to and processing in countries that may have different data protection laws.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
